Mike the Friendican

I really thought that Friendica's "global directory" would be short lived and Google would start indexing people on the web. But you're right - G+ came along and that hope died.

I just wish for a way to get to webfinger from full name. If I'm looking for an ex co-worker, I'm only going to have a name. That doesn't give me a webbie (my new term for webfinger address). That's why I created a central directory even though it's a decentralised service. I don't see another solution short of every hub storing the entire directory and passing around deltas, which presents a scaling problem.

By the way, every Friendica node has a webfinger and 'probe' utility to poke around and try and find a communication protocol we can use. That lets us follow email conversations and blogs as if they were fedsocweb people. It's a diagnostic tool and not pretty but you can go to yoursite/probe and see what it says for a given webbie/url/whatever.
